MUSIC

Music at AK ENM is designed with structure before genre.
Grounded in classical, contemporary, and experimental composition,
we work with density of sound, meaning of silence, and perception of time.

Our music production operates through two parallel frameworks.
One focuses on research‑driven artistic creation (PURE / CONTEMPORARY),
the other on music that circulates and functions within popular culture (POP / CULTURE).
These domains remain distinct, yet expand from a shared foundation.

PURE / CONTEMPORARY

Creation rooted in the language of contemporary and classical music.
We explore sonic structure and temporality, developing sound practices
expandable into film, performance, and exhibition contexts.

Director
Sung Hie Ahn — Composer · Professor

POP / CULTURE

Music production operating within contemporary popular culture.
We develop albums, performances, and music‑based visual content,
establishing structural and aesthetic standards before distribution.
